What is paddleboarding?


Stand Up Paddleboarding (SUP) is the fastest growing water sport in the UK. Think of it as standing on an inflatable surfboard and using a long paddle to get around! Along with traditional paddleboaring you can also try a range of activities.

SUP Surf

Similar to traditional surfing except you don't lie prone to catch a wave. An excellent alternative for those with hip or shoulder injuries.

SUP Yoga

Enjoy all the benefits of Yoga but outside on a beautiful body of water. Can only be done on calm waters like lakes or bays.

SUP Fishing

Access shallow waters at low cost, a great way for anglers to extend their fishing grounds.
